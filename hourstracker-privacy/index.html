Privacy Policy – Hours Tracker

Last updated: 2026-01-24

Home Support

Hours Tracker respects your privacy. We keep personal data collection to a minimum and use third-party services only where necessary.

Data Collection

  • All data is stored locally on your device. Your time entries and history are not sent to any external servers by the app itself.
  • We show non-personalized ads via Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). We request that ads are not tailored using your personal data. The ad network may still receive basic device and usage information needed to serve and measure ads.
  • If you upgrade to the paid version, ads are removed and we stop requesting ads from AdMob, so no ad network data is sent after you upgrade.

Permissions

  • The app does not request access to your location, contacts, or photos.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact support at support-hourstracker@proton.me.

Note: Third-party services may process limited data as described above to operate and measure ads or network requests.
