Royal Flush respects your privacy. We keep data collection to a minimum and use third-party services only where necessary.

Data Collection

  • In-app purchases, if offered, are handled by Apple StoreKit.

Health Data

  • Royal Flush does not write data to Apple Health by default.
  • If future versions offer optional integrations, this policy will be updated before rollout.

Notifications

  • If you enable reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device.

Permissions

  • The app does not request access to your location, contacts, photos, or microphone.
